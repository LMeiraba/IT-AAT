Our Journey

ChakHao wasn't built by a single person; it was born from a collective dream. We are a team of childhood friends who grew up believing that the best conversations happen around the dinner table.

Frustrated by the lack of authentic flavors in the city, we decided to open our own kitchen. What started as a small street stall has grown into the restaurant you see today, but our mission remains the same: to make every bite unforgettable.
